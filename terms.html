Terms of Service

The basics of how we work together. This is a demo site — final terms will be confirmed with the business.

1. Our service

KiwiSafe Test & Tag provides electrical appliance testing and tagging and related inspection services to AS/NZS 3760 in Christchurch and greater Canterbury. Testing confirms that items pass the relevant checks at the time of inspection.

2. Quotes and pricing

Quotes are based on the information you provide about the number and type of items and the site. If the actual scope differs on the day, we'll talk it through before any change to price.

3. Access and site readiness

To test efficiently we need reasonable access to the items and to power outlets. Equipment in use, locked away or unavailable on the day may need a follow-up visit.

4. Results and records

You'll receive a register of items tested, with pass results tagged and any failed or removed items noted. A passed tag reflects the item's condition at the time of testing and does not guarantee future performance.

5. Liability

We carry out testing with reasonable care and skill. To the extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to re-performing the affected testing. Nothing in these terms limits rights you have under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993.

6. Payment

Invoices are payable by the date shown on the invoice. Please raise any query about an invoice with us promptly so we can sort it.

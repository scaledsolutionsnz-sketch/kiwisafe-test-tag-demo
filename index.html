What we test

Electrical safety testing, sorted on one visit.

From a single office floor to a full construction site, we cover the testing your health-and-safety obligations call for under AS/NZS 3760.

Appliance test & tag

Leads, power tools, kettles, chargers and office gear — visually checked, electrically tested and tagged with a clear next-test date.

RCD testing

Trip-time and push-button testing on safety switches so your boards cut power fast when it counts.

Switchboard checks

A visual and functional check of boards and distribution to flag wear, heat damage or loose fittings early.

New-to-service testing

Brand-new and hired-in equipment tested and tagged before it goes into use on site.

Site & construction testing

Scheduled three-monthly testing for construction and demolition sites, where the rules are tightest.

Asset registers & reminders

A full itemised register after every visit, plus a reminder when your next test is due so nothing lapses.

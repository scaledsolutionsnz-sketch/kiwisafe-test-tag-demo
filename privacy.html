Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us. This is a demo site — the final policy will be confirmed with the business.

What we collect

When you enquire we collect the details you give us — your name, contact details, site location and a description of the job — so we can respond and provide a quote.

How we use it

We use your information only to reply to your enquiry, carry out testing, keep your asset register, and remind you when your next test is due. We don't sell your information to anyone.

Sharing

We don't share your details with third parties except where it's needed to deliver the service (for example a scheduling or email tool) or where the law requires it.

Storage and security

Your information is kept only as long as needed for the service and our records, and we take reasonable steps to keep it secure.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the information we hold about you and ask us to correct it. Get in touch and we'll help.
